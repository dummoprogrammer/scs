Stupid Creeper Simulator is probably the worst video game you've ever seen thus far, but don't worry, it only gets worse.

On October 27, 2017, this game's creator (wow that's me! :-D) started a comic series based on the Creeper mob from Minecraft. He named it The Life Of Jimmy Creeper.
I'm going to start using first person pronouns here since I find writing an autobiographic text in the third person very disconcerting.
I was only ten when I started this series, but I was starting to get into graphic novels like Big Nate and Captain Underpants at the time, and this comic was my best take on an imitation of that.
Just a couple of weeks after starting I moved to a new neighborhood and a new school. The art teacher at that new school was very nice, and after some time I showed her my fresh comic series.
She was fascinated and genuinely loved the content. Shortly after a comic creator buzz rolled around the school, but by then I had graduated to junior high.

I was very slowly losing interest of making the series over the years, and eventually decided to discontinue the series in 2020, however a series of similar looking doodles seemed to become my new pastime.
I was even able to make a few friends over it.
These drawings seemed to perfectly reflect every stupid and random thought in my head.

On August 24, 2022, I decided to take another crack at a JavaScript platformer engine I failed to make in 2021.
This time it worked.
I posted the progress I made on a new channel I named "The Coding Creeper", and eventually decided to revitalize the Jimmy Creeper series once and for all.

After six years, Jimmy Creeper has come from a comic strip to a video game.
And it's only getting dumber from here.
